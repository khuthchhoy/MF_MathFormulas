Functions: $f,u,v$
Independent variable: $x$
Derivative of a function: $y'(x),f'(x)$
Real constant: $C$
Differential of function $y=f(x)$: $dy$
Differential of $x$: $dx$
Small change in $x$: $\Delta x$
Small change in $y$: $\Delta y$

  1. $\mathrm{d}y = y'\mathrm{d}x$
  2. $f\Big(x+\Delta x\Big) = f(x) + f'(x)\Delta x$
  3. Small Change in $y$ $$\Delta y = f\Big(x+\Delta x\Big) - f(x)$$
  4. $\mathrm{d}(u+v) = \mathrm{d}u + \mathrm{d}v$
  5. $\mathrm{d}(u-v) = \mathrm{d}u - \mathrm{d}v$
  6. $\mathrm{d}(Cu) = C\mathrm{d}u$
  7. $\mathrm{d}(uv) = v\mathrm{d}u + u\mathrm{d}v$
  8. $\mathrm{d}\Big( \dfrac{u}{v}Big) = \dfrac{v\mathrm{d}u - u\mathrm{d}v}{v^2}$
