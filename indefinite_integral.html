Functions: $f,g,u,v$
Independent variables: $x,t,\xi$
Indefinite integral of a function: $\displaystyle\int f(x)dx, \int g(x)dx,...$
Derivative of a function: $y'(x),f'(x), F'(x),...$
Real constants: $C,a,b,c,d,k$
Natural numbers: $m,n,i,j$

Indefinite Integral

  1. $\displaystyle\int f(x)dx = F(x) + C$ if $F'(x) = f(x)$
  2. $\Big( \displaystyle\int f(x)dx \Big)'dx = f(x)$
  3. $\displaystyle\int kf(x)dx = k\int f(x)dx$
  4. $\displaystyle\int\Big[f(x) + g(x) \Big] dx = \int f(x)dx + \int g(x)dx$
  5. $\displaystyle\int\Big[ f(x) - g(x)\Big] dx = \int f(x)dx - \int g(x) dx$
  6. $\displaystyle\int f(ax)dx = \dfrac{1}{a}F(ax) + C$
  7. $\displaystyle\int f(ax+b) dx = \dfrac{1}{a}F(ax+b) + C$
  8. $\displaystyle\int f(x)f'(x) dx = \dfrac{1}{2}f^2(x) + C$
  9. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{f'(x)}{f(x)} dx = \ln\Big| f(x)\Big| + C$
  10. Method of Substitution
    If $x=u(t)$ then, $$\displaystyle\int f(x) dx = \int f\Big(u(t) \Big)u'(t)dt$$
  11. Integration by Parts $$\displaystyle\int udv = uv - \int vdu,$$ where $u(x),v(x)$ are differentiable functions.

Integrals of Rational Functions

  1. $\displaystyle\int adx = ax + C$
  2. $\displaystyle\int xdx = \dfrac{x^2}{2} + C$
  3. $\displaystyle\int x^2dx = \dfrac{x^3}{3} + C$
  4. $\displaystyle\int x^p dx = \dfrac{x^{p+1}}{p+1} + C,\,p\ne -1$
  5. $\displaystyle\int \Big( ax+b\Big)^ndx = \dfrac{(ax+b)^{n+1}}{a(n+1)} + C,\,n\ne -1$
  6. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x} = \ln|x| + C$
  7. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{ax+b} = \dfrac{1}{a}\ln|ax+b| + C$
  8. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{ax+b}{cx+d}dx = \dfrac{a}{c}x + \dfrac{bc-ad}{c^2}\ln|cx+d| + C$
  9. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{(x+a)(x+b)} = \dfrac{1}{a-b}\ln\Big|\frac{x+b}{x+a} \Big| + C,\,a\ne b$
  10. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{xdx}{a+bx} = \dfrac{1}{b^2}\Big(a+bx-a\ln\big|a+bx\big| \Big) + C$
  11. $\displaystyle\int\dfrac{ x^2dx}{a+bx} = \dfrac{1}{b^3}\Big[\dfrac{1}{2}(a+bx)^2-2a(a+bx)+a^2\ln|a+bx| \Big] + C$
  12. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{dx}{x(a+bx)} = \dfrac{1}{a}+\ln\Big| \dfrac{a+bx}{x}\Big| + C$
  13. $\displaystyle\int\dfrac{ dx}{x^2(a+bx)} = -\dfrac{1}{ax}+\dfrac{b}{a^2}\ln\Big|\dfrac{a+bx}{x} \Big| + C$
  14. $\displaystyle\int\dfrac{x dx}{(a+bx)^2} = \dfrac{1}{b^2}\Big(\ln|a+bx|+\dfrac{a}{a+bx} \Big) + C$
  15. $\displaystyle\int\frac{x^{2}dx}{(a+bx)^{2}}=\frac{1}{b^{3}}(a+bx-2a\ln|a+bx|-\frac{a^{2}}{a+bx})+C$
  16. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{x(a+bx)^{2}}=\frac{1}{a(a+bx)}+\frac{1}{a^{2}}\ln\left|\frac{a+bx}{x}\right|+C$
  17. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{x^{2}-1}=\frac{1}{2}\ln\left|\frac{x-1}{x+1}\right|+C$
  18. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{1-x^{2}}=\frac{1}{2}\ln\left|\frac{1+x}{1-x}\right|+C$
  19. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{a^{2}-x^{2}}=\frac{1}{2a}\ln\left|\frac{a+x}{a-x}\right|+C$
  20. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{x^{2}-a^{2}}=\frac{1}{2a}\ln\left|\frac{x-a}{x+a}\right|+C$
  21. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{1+x^{2}}=\tan^{-1}x+C$
  22. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{a^{2}+x^{2}}=\frac{1}{a}\tan^{-1}\frac{x}{a}+C$
  23. $\displaystyle\int\frac{xdx}{x^{2}+a^{2}}=\frac{1}{2}\ln(x^{2}+a^{2})+C$
  24. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{a+bx^{2}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ab}}\tan^{-1}\left(x\sqrt{\frac{b}{a}}\right)+C, ab > 0$
  25. $\displaystyle\int\frac{xdx}{a+bx^{2}}=\frac{1}{2b}\ln|x^{2}+\frac{a}{b}|+C$
  26. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{x(a+bx^{2})}=\frac{1}{2a}\ln\left|\frac{x^{2}}{a+bx^{2}}\right|+C$
  27. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{a^{2}-b^{2}x^{2}}=\frac{1}{2ab}\ln\left|\frac{a+bx}{a-bx}\right|+C$
  28. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{ax^{2}+bx+c}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{b^{2}-4ac}}\ln\left|\frac{2ax+b-\sqrt{b^{2}-4ac}}{2ax+b+\sqrt{b^{2}-4ac}}\right|+C, b^{2}-4ac>0$
  29. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{ax^{2}+bx+c}=\frac{2}{\sqrt{4ac-b^{2}}}\arctan\frac{2ax+b}{\sqrt{4ac-b^{2}}}+C, b^{2}-4ac<0$

Integrals of Irrational Functions

  1. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{\sqrt{ax+b}}=\frac{2}{a}\sqrt{ax+b}+C$
  2. $\displaystyle\int\sqrt{ax+b}dx=\frac{2}{3a}(ax+b)^{\frac{3}{2}}+C$
  3. $\displaystyle\int\frac{xdx}{\sqrt{ax+b}}=\frac{2(ax-2b)}{3a^{2}}\sqrt{ax+b}+C$
  4. $\displaystyle\int x\sqrt{ax+b}dx=\frac{2(3ax-2b)}{15a^{2}}(ax+b)^{\frac{3}{2}}+C$
  5. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{(x+c)\sqrt{ax+b}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{b-ac}}\ln\left|\frac{\sqrt{ax+b}-\sqrt{b-ac}}{\sqrt{ax+b}+\sqrt{b-ac}}\right|+C, b-ac \gt 0$
  6. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{(x+c)\sqrt{ax+b}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ac-b}}\arctan\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{ac-b}}+C, b-ac\lt 0$
  7. $\displaystyle\int\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{cx+d}}dx=\frac{1}{c}\sqrt{(ax+b)(cx+d)}-\frac{ad-bc}{c\sqrt{ac}}\ln\left|\sqrt{a(cx+d)}+\sqrt{c(ax+b)}\right|+C, a\gt 0$
  8. $\displaystyle\int\sqrt{\frac{ax+b}{cx+d}}dx=\frac{1}{c}\sqrt{(ax+b)(cx+d)}-\frac{ad-bc}{c\sqrt{ac}}\arctan\sqrt{\frac{a(cx+d)}{c(ax+b)}}+C, (a\ lt 0, c\gt 0)$
  9. $\displaystyle\int x^{2}\sqrt{a+bx}dx=\frac{2(8a^{2}-12abx+15b^{2}x^{2})}{105b^{3}}\sqrt{(a+bx)^{3}}+C$
  10. $\displaystyle\int\frac{x^{2}dx}{\sqrt{a+bx}}=\frac{2(8a^{2}-4abx+3b^{2}x^{2})}{15b^{3}}\sqrt{a+bx}+C$
  11. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{x\sqrt{a+bx}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\ln\left|\frac{\sqrt{a+bx}-\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{a+bx}+\sqrt{a}}\right|+C, a\gt 0$
