Functions: $f,g,u,v$
Independent variables: $x,t,\xi$
Indefinite integral of a function: $\displaystyle\int f(x)dx, \int g(x)dx,...$
Derivative of a function: $y'(x),f'(x), F'(x),...$
Real constants: $C,a,b,c,d,k$
Natural numbers: $m,n,i,j$

Indefinite Integral

  1. $\displaystyle\int f(x)dx = F(x) + C$ if $F'(x) = f(x)$
  2. $\Big( \displaystyle\int f(x)dx \Big)'dx = f(x)$
  3. $\displaystyle\int kf(x)dx = k\int f(x)dx$
  4. $\displaystyle\int\Big[f(x) + g(x) \Big] dx = \int f(x)dx + \int g(x)dx$
  5. $\displaystyle\int\Big[ f(x) - g(x)\Big] dx = \int f(x)dx - \int g(x) dx$
  6. $\displaystyle\int f(ax)dx = \dfrac{1}{a}F(ax) + C$
  7. $\displaystyle\int f(ax+b) dx = \dfrac{1}{a}F(ax+b) + C$
  8. $\displaystyle\int f(x)f'(x) dx = \dfrac{1}{2}f^2(x) + C$
  9. $\displaystyle\int \dfrac{f'(x)}{f(x)} dx = \ln\Big| f(x)\Big| + C$
  10. Method of Substitution
    If $x=u(t)$ then, $$\displaystyle\int f(x) dx = \int f\Big(u(t) \Big)u'(t)dt$$
  11. Integration by Parts $$\displaystyle\int udv = uv - \int vdu,$$ where $u(x),v(x)$ are differentiable functions.

Reduction Formulas

  1. $\displaystyle\int x^{n}e^{mx}dx=\frac{1}{m}x^{n}e^{mx}-\frac{n}{m}\int x^{n-1}e^{mx}dx$

  3. $\displaystyle\int \sinh^{n}xdx=\frac{1}{n}\sinh^{n-1}x \cosh x-\frac{n-1}{n}\int \sinh^{n-2}xdx$
  4. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{\sinh^{n}x}=-\frac{\cosh x}{(n-1)\sinh^{n-1}x}-\frac{n-2}{n-1}\int\frac{dx}{\sinh^{n-2}x}, n\ne1$

  8. $\displaystyle\int \sinh^{n}x \cosh^{m}xdx=\frac{\sinh^{n-1}x \cosh^{m+1}x}{n+m}-\frac{n-1}{n+m}\int \sinh^{n-2}x \cosh^{m}xdx$

  10. $\displaystyle\int \coth^{n}xdx=-\frac{1}{n-1}\coth^{n-1}x+\int \coth^{n-2}xdx, n\ne1$
  11. $\displaystyle\int \operatorname{sech}^{n}xdx=\frac{\operatorname{sech}^{n-2}x \tanh x}{n-1}+\frac{n-2}{n-1}\int \operatorname{sech}^{n-2}xdx, n\ne1$
  12. $\displaystyle\int \sin^{n}xdx=-\frac{1}{n}\sin^{n-1}x \cos x+\frac{n-1}{n}\int \sin^{n-2}xdx$
  13. $\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{\sin^{n}x}=-\frac{\cos x}{(n-1)\sin^{n-1}x}+\frac{n-2}{n-1}\int\frac{dx}{\sin^{n-2}x}, n\ne1$
  14. $\displaystyle\int \cos^{n}xdx=\frac{1}{n}\sin x \cos^{n-1}x+\frac{n-1}{n}\int \cos^{n-2}xdx$
