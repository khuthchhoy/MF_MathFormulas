Variables: $x,y,z$
Real numbers: $\begin{cases}a,b,c,d \\ a_1,a_2,...,a_n \end{cases},\,m,n$
Determinants: $D,D_x,D_y,D_z$

  1. Inequalities and Interval Notations $$\begin{array}{|l|l|} \hline \\\\ \text{Inequality} & \text{Interval Notation} \\ \hline \\\\ a \le x\le b& [a,b] \\ \hline \\\\ a \lt x\le b & (a,b] \\ \hline \\\\ a\le x\lt b & [a,b) \\ \hline \\ \\ a \lt x\le b & (a,b) \\ \hline \\\\ -\infty\lt x\le b,\,x\le b& (-\infty,b]\\ \hline\\\\ -\infty\lt x\lt b,\,x\lt b& (-\infty,b)\\ \hline\\\\ a\le x\lt \infty,\,x\gt a& [a,\infty) \\ \hline\\\\ a\lt x\lt \infty,\,x\gt a& (a,\infty) \end{array}$$
  2. If $a \gt b$, then $b \lt a$.
  3. If $a \gt b$, then $a - b \gt 0$ or $b - a \lt 0$.
  4. If $a \gt b$, then $a + c \gt b + c$.
  5. If $a \gt b$, then $a - c \gt b - c$.
  6. If $a \gt b$ and $c \gt d$, then $a + c \gt b + d$.
  7. If $a \gt b$ and $c \gt d$, then $a - d \gt b - c$.
  8. If $a \gt b$ and $m \gt 0$, then $ma \gt mb$.
  9. If $a \gt b$ and $m \gt 0$, then $\dfrac{a}{m} \gt \dfrac{b}{m}$.
  10. If $a \gt b$ and $m \lt 0$, then $ma \lt mb$.
  11. If $a \gt b$ and $m \lt 0$, then $\dfrac{a}{m} \lt \dfrac{b}{m}$.
  12. If $0 \lt a \lt b$ and $n \gt 0$, then $a^{n} \lt b^{n}$.
  13. If $0 \lt a \lt b$ and $n \lt 0$, then $a^{n} \gt b^{n}$.
  14. If $0 \lt a \lt b$, then $\sqrt[n]{a} \lt \sqrt[n]{b}$.
  15. Arithmetic Mean-Geometric Mean (AM-GM) Inequality: $$\sqrt{ab} \le \dfrac{a+b}{2}$$ where $a \gt 0, b \gt 0$. The equality holds if $a = b$.
  16. Property of reciprocals: $$a + \dfrac{1}{a} \ge 2$$ where $a \gt 0$. The equality holds if $a = 1$.
  17. General Arithmetic Mean-Geometric Mean (AM-GM) Inequality: $$\sqrt[n]{a_{1}a_{2}\dots a_{n}} \le \dfrac{a_{1}+a_{2}+\dots+a_{n}}{n}$$ where $a_{1}, a_{2}, \dots, a_{n} \gt 0$.
  18. If $ax + b \gt 0$ and $a \gt 0$, then $x \gt -\dfrac{b}{a}$.
  19. If $ax + b \gt 0$ and $a \lt 0$, then $x \lt -\dfrac{b}{a}$.
  20. Quadratic Inequality $ax^{2} + bx + c \gt 0$:
    • If $D \gt 0$ and $a \gt 0$, then $x \lt x_{1}$ or $x \gt x_{2}$.
    • If $D \gt 0$ and $a \lt 0$, then $x_{1} \lt x \lt x_{2}$.
    • If $D = 0$ and $a \gt 0$, then $x \lt x_{1}$ or $x \gt x_{1}$ (all $x \ne x_{1}$).
    • If $D = 0$ and $a \lt 0$, then $x \in \emptyset$.
    • If $D \lt 0$ and $a \gt 0$, then $-\infty \lt x \lt \infty$.
    • If $D \lt 0$ and $a \lt 0$, then $x \in \emptyset$.
  21. Triangle Inequality: $$|a+b| \le |a|+|b|$$
  22. If $|x| \lt a$, then $-a \lt x \lt a$, where $a \gt 0$.
  23. If $|x| \gt a$, then $x \lt -a$ and $x \gt a$, where $a \gt 0$.
  24. If $x^{2} \lt a$, then $|x| \lt \sqrt{a}$, where $a \gt 0$.
  25. If $x^{2} \gt a$, then $|x| \gt \sqrt{a}$, where $a \gt 0$.
  26. If $\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} \gt 0$, then $\begin{cases}f(x) \cdot g(x) \gt 0 \\ g(x) \ne 0\end{cases}$.
  27. If $\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} \lt 0$, then $\begin{cases}f(x) \cdot g(x) \lt 0 \\ g(x) \ne 0\end{cases}$.
