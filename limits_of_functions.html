Functions: $f(x),g(x)$
Argument: $x$
Real constants: $a,k$

  1. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}\Big[f(x) + g(x) \Big] = \lim_{x\to a} f(x) + \lim_{x\to a} g(x)$
  2. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}\Big[f(x) - g(x) \Big] = \lim_{x\to a} f(x) - \lim_{x\to a} g(x)$
  3. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}\Big[f(x) \cdot g(x) \Big] = \lim_{x\to a} f(x) \cdot \lim_{x\to a} g(x)$
  4. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \dfrac{\lim_{x\to a} f(x)}{\lim_{x\to a} g(x)}$ if $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} g(x)\ne 0$
  5. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}\Big[kf(x)\Big] = k\lim_{x\to a} f(x)$
  6. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a}f\Big(g(x) \Big) = f\Big(\lim_{x\to a} g(x)\Big)$
  7. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} f(x) = f(a)$, if the function $f(x)$ is continuous at $x = a$.
  8. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\sin x}{x} = 1$
  9. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\tan x}{x} = 1$
  10. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\sin^{-1} x}{x} = 1$
  11. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\tan^{-1} x}{x} = 1$
  12. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\ln(1 + x)}{x} = 1$
  13. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to \infty} \Big(1+ \dfrac{1}{x}\Big)^x = e$
  14. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to \infty} \Big(1+ \dfrac{k}{x}\Big)^x = e^k$
  15. $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to 0} a^x = 1$
