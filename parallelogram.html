Side of a parallelogram: $a,b$
Diagonals: $d_1,d_2$
Consecutive angles: $\alpha,\beta$
Angle between the diagonals: $varphi$
Altitude: $h$
Perimeter: $L$
Area: $S$

1. $\alpha+\beta=180^\circ $

2. $d_1^2+d_2^2=2\Big(a^2+b^2\Big)$

3. Altitude:$$h=b\sin \alpha=b\sin \beta$$

4. Perimeter:$$L=2(a+b)$$

5. Area: $$\begin{aligned} S&=ah=ab\sin \alpha\\ &=\dfrac{1}{2}d_1d_2\sin \varphi\end{aligned}$$
