Legs of a right triangle: $a,b$
Hypotenuse: $c$
Altitude: $h$
Medians: $m_a,m_b,m_c$
Angles: $\alpha,\beta$
Radius of circumscribed circle: $R$
Radius of inscribed circle: $r$
Area: $S$

  1. $\alpha + \beta = 90^\circ$
  2. Sine and Cosine relationship in a right triangle: $$ \sin \alpha = \dfrac{a}{c} = \cos \beta $$
  3. Cosine and Sine relationship in a right triangle: $$ \cos \alpha = \dfrac{b}{c} = \sin \beta $$
  4. Tangent and Cotangent relationship in a right triangle: $$ \tan \alpha = \dfrac{a}{b} = \cot \beta $$
  5. Cotangent and Tangent relationship in a right triangle: $$ \cot \alpha = \dfrac{b}{a} = \tan \beta $$
  6. Secant and Cosecant relationship in a right triangle: $$ \sec \alpha = \dfrac{c}{b} = \operatorname{cosec} \beta $$
  7. Cosecant and Secant relationship in a right triangle: $$ \operatorname{cosec} \alpha = \dfrac{c}{a} = \sec \beta $$
  8. Pythagorean Theorem: $$ a^{2} + b^{2} = c^{2} $$
  9. Projections of the legs onto the hypotenuse: $$ a^{2} = fc, \quad b^{2} = gc $$ where $f$ and $g$ are projections of the legs $a$ and $b$, respectively, onto the hypotenuse $c$.
  10. 165. Altitude from the right angle: $$ h^{2} = fg $$ where $h$ is the altitude from the right angle to the hypotenuse.
  11. Medians to the legs: $$ m_{a}^{2} = b^{2} - \dfrac{a^{2}}{4}, \quad m_{b}^{2} = a^{2} - \dfrac{b^{2}}{4} $$ where $m_{a}$ and $m_{b}$ are the medians to the legs $a$ and $b$.
  12. Median to the hypotenuse: $$ m_{c} = \dfrac{c}{2} $$ where $m_{c}$ is the median to the hypotenuse $c$.
  13. Circumradius of a right triangle: $$ R = \dfrac{c}{2} = m_{c} $$
  14. Inradius of a right triangle: $$ r = \dfrac{a + b - c}{2} = \dfrac{ab}{a + b + c} $$
  15. Area relationship in a right triangle: $$ ab = ch $$
  16. Area of a right triangle: $$ S = \dfrac{ab}{2} = \dfrac{ch}{2} $$
