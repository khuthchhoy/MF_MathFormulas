Quadratic Equation $ax^2+bx+c=0$

The solutions: $x=\dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$

If $a$, $b$, $c$ are real and if $D=b^2-4ac$ is the discriminant, then the rootare

  1. real and unequal if $D > 0$
  2. real and equal if $D = 0$
  3. complex conjugate if $D < 0$

If $x_1$, $x_2$ are the roots, then $x_1+x_2=-\dfrac{b}{a}$ and $x_1x_2=\dfrac{c}{a}$.

Cubic Equation: $x^3+a_1x^2+a_2x+a_3=0$

Let $$\begin{aligned} &Q=\dfrac{3a_2-a_1^2}{9}\\ &R=\dfrac{9a_1a_2-27a_3-2a_1^3}{54}\\ &S=\sqrt[3]{R+\sqrt{Q^3+R^2}} \\ &T=\sqrt[3]{R-\sqrt{Q^3+R^2}} \end{aligned}$$ where $ST=-Q$.

Solutions: $$\begin{cases}x_1=S+T-\dfrac{1}{3}a_1\\ x_2=-\dfrac{1}{2}(S+T)-\dfrac{1}{3}a_1+\dfrac{1}{2}i\sqrt{3}(S-T)\\ x_3=-\dfrac{1}{2}(S+T)-\dfrac{1}{3}a_1-\dfrac{1}{2}i\sqrt{3}(S-T)\end{cases}$$

If $a_1, a_2, a_3$ are real and if $D=Q^3+R^2$ is the discriminant, then

  1. one root is real and two are complex conjugate if $D > 0$
  2. all roots are real and at least two are equal if $D = 0$
  3. all roots are real and unequal if $D \lt 0$

If $D \lt 0$, computation is simplified by using of trigonometry.

Solutions: $$\text{if } D\lt 0: \begin{cases} x_1=2\sqrt{-Q} \cos\left(\frac{1}{3}\theta\right) - \frac{1}{3}a_1\\ x_2=2\sqrt{-Q}\cos\left( \frac{1}{3}\theta + 120^{\circ} \right) - \frac{1}{3}a_1\\ x_3=2\sqrt{-Q}\cos\left( \frac{1}{3}\theta + 240^{\circ}\right) - \frac{1}{3}a \end{cases}$$ where $\cos\theta=\dfrac{R}{\sqrt{-Q^3}}$

$x_1+x_2+x_3=-a_1$, $x_1x_2+x_2x_3+x_3x_1=a_2$, $x_1x_2x_3=-a_3$ where $x_1, x_2, x_3$ are the three roots.

Quartic Equation: $x^4+a_1x^3+a_2x^2+a_3x+a_4=0$

Let $y_1$ be a real root of cubic equation: $$y^3-a_2y^2+\left( a_1a_3-4a_4\right)y+\left(4a_2a_4-a_3^2-a_1^2a_4\right)=0,\,(1)$$ The four roots of the quartic equation are the four roots of the following equation: $$z^2+\dfrac{1}{2}\left(a_1\pm \sqrt{a_1^2-4a_2+4y_1}\right)z+\dfrac{1}{2}\left( y_1\pm\sqrt{y_1^2-4a_4} \right)=0,\, (2)$$

Suppose that all roots of (1) are real, then computation is simplified by using the particular real root that produces all real coefficients in the quadratic equation (2).

$$\begin{cases} x_1+x_2+x_3+x_4=-a\\ x_1x_2+x_2x_3+x_3x_4+x_4x_1+x_1x_3+x_2x_4=a_2\\ x_1x_2x_3+x_2x_3x_4+x_1x_2x_4+x_1x_3x_4=-a_3\\ x_1x_2x_3x_4=a_4 \end{cases}$$ where $x_1,x_2,x_3,x_4$ are the four roots.
