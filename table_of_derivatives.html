Independent variable: $x$
Real constants: $C,a,b,c$
Natural number: $n$

  1. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}(C) = 0$
  2. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}(x) = 1$
  3. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}(ax + b) = a$
  4. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\left(ax^2 + bx + c\right) = ax + b$
  5. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\left( x^n\right) = nx^{n-1}$
  6. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\left( x^{-n}\right) = -\dfrac{n}{x^{n+1}}$
  7. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big(\dfrac{1}{x} \Big) = -\dfrac{1}{x^2}$
  8. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\left(\sqrt{x} \right) = \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}$
  9. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big(\sqrt[n]{x} \Big) = \dfrac{1}{n\sqrt[n]{x^{n-1}}}$
  10. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big(\ln x \Big) = \dfrac{1}{x}$
  11. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big(\log_a x \Big) = \dfrac{1}{x\ln a},\,a\gt 0,\, a\ne 1$
  12. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( a^x\Big) = a^x\ln a,\,a\gt 0,\,a\ne 1$
  13. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( e^x\Big) = e^x$
  14. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \sin x\Big) = \cos x$
  15. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \cos x\Big) = -\sin x$
  16. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \tan x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{\cos^2 x} = \sec^2 x$
  17. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \cot x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{\sin^2 x} = -\csc^2 x$
  18. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \sec x\Big) = \tan x\cdot \sec x$
  19. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \csc x\Big) = -\cot x\cdot \csc x$
  20. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big(\arcsin x \Big) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$
  21. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \arccos x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$
  22. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \arctan x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{1+x^2}$
  23. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arccot} x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{1+x^2}$
  24. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arcsec} x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2-1}}$
  25. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arccsc} x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2-1}}$
  26. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \sinh x\Big) = \cosh x$
  27. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \cosh x\Big) = \sinh x$
  28. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \tanh x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{\cosh^2 x}=\operatorname{sech}^2 x$
  29. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \coth x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{\sinh^2 x}=-\operatorname{csch}^2 x$
  30. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{sech} x\Big) = -\operatorname{sech} x\cdot \tanh x$
  31. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{csch} x\Big) = -\operatorname{csch} x\cdot \coth x$
  32. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arcsinh} x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}$
  33. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arccosh} x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}$
  34. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arctanh} x\Big) = \dfrac{1}{1-x^2},\,|x|\lt 1$
  35. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( \operatorname{arccoth} x\Big) = -\dfrac{1}{x^2-1},\,|x|\gt 1$
  36. $\dfrac{\mathrm{d} }{\mathrm{d} x}\Big( u^v\Big) = vu^{v-1}\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d} x}+u^v\ln u\cdot \dfrac{\mathrm{d} v}{\mathrm{d} x}$
