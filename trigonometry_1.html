Most Important Formulas

1. $\sin^2 \alpha + \cos^2 \alpha = 1$

2. $\sec^2 \alpha - \tan^2 \alpha = 1$

3. $\csc^2 \alpha - \cot^2 \alpha = 1$

4. $\tan^2\alpha=\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha}$

5. $\cot\alpha=\dfrac{\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}$

6. $\tan\alpha \cdot \cot\alpha=1$

7. $\sec\alpha=\dfrac{1}{\cos\alpha}$

8. $\cosec \alpha=\dfrac{1}{\sin\alpha}$

Periodicity of Trigonometric Functions

1. $\sin(\alpha \pm 2\pi n) = \sin \alpha$, period $2\pi$ or $360^\circ$

2. $\cos(\alpha \pm 2\pi n) = \cos \alpha$, period $2\pi$ or $360^\circ$

3. $\tan(\alpha \pm \pi n) = \tan \alpha$, period $\pi$ or $180^\circ$

4. $\cot(\alpha \pm \pi n) = \cot \alpha$, period $\pi$ or $180^\circ$

Relations between Trigonometric Functions

2. $\sin \alpha = \pm\sqrt{1-\cos^2\alpha} $
$= \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{2}(1-\cos 2\alpha)} = 2 \cos^2(\dfrac{\alpha}{2}-\dfrac{\pi}{4})-1$
$= \dfrac{2 \tan\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1+\tan^2\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

2. $\cos \alpha = \pm\sqrt{1-\sin^2\alpha}$
$= \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{2}(1+\cos 2\alpha)} = 2 \cos^2\dfrac{\alpha}{2}-1$
$= \dfrac{1-\tan^2\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1+\tan^2\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

3. $\tan \alpha = \dfrac{\sin \alpha}{\cos \alpha} = \pm\sqrt{\sec^2\alpha-1}$
$= \dfrac{\sin 2\alpha}{1+\cos 2\alpha} = \dfrac{1-\cos 2\alpha}{\sin 2\alpha}$
$=\pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1-\cos 2\alpha}{1+\cos 2\alpha}} = \dfrac{2 \tan\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1-\tan^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

4. $\cot \alpha = \dfrac{\cos \alpha}{\sin \alpha} = \pm\sqrt{\csc^{2}\alpha-1} $
$= \dfrac{1+\cos 2\alpha}{\sin 2\alpha} = \dfrac{\sin 2\alpha}{1-\cos 2\alpha}$
$= \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1+\cos 2\alpha}{1-\cos 2\alpha}} = \dfrac{1-\tan^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{2 \tan\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

5. $\sec \alpha = \dfrac{1}{\cos \alpha} = \pm\sqrt{1+\tan^{2}\alpha} = \dfrac{1+\tan^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1-\tan^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

6. $\csc \alpha = \dfrac{1}{\sin \alpha} = \pm\sqrt{1+\cot^{2}\alpha} = \dfrac{1+\tan^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{2 \tan\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

Addition and Subtraction Formulas

1. $\sin(\alpha+\beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta + \sin \beta \cos \alpha$

2. $\sin(\alpha-\beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta - \sin \beta \cos \alpha$

3. $\cos(\alpha+\beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta - \sin \alpha \sin \beta$

4. $\cos(\alpha-\beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta + \sin \alpha \sin \beta$

5. $\tan(\alpha+\beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}{1 - \tan \alpha \tan \beta}$

6. $\tan(\alpha-\beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha - \tan \beta}{1 + \tan \alpha \tan \beta}$

7. $\cot(\alpha+\beta) = \dfrac{1 - \tan \alpha \tan \beta}{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}$

8. $\cot(\alpha-\beta) = \dfrac{1 + \tan \alpha \tan \beta}{\tan \alpha - \tan \beta}$

Double Angle Formulas

1. $\sin 2\alpha = 2 \sin \alpha \cdot \cos \alpha$

2. $\cos 2\alpha = \cos^2 \alpha - \sin^2 \alpha = 1 - 2 \sin^2 \alpha = 2 \cos^2 \alpha - 1$

3. $\tan 2\alpha = \dfrac{2 \tan \alpha}{1 - \tan^2 \alpha} = \dfrac{2}{\cot \alpha - \tan \alpha}$

4. $\cot 2\alpha = \dfrac{\cot^2 \alpha - 1}{2 \cot \alpha} = \dfrac{\cot \alpha - \tan \alpha}{2}$

Multiple Angle Formulas

1. $\sin 3\alpha = 3 \sin \alpha - 4 \sin^3 \alpha = 3 \cos^2 \alpha \cdot \sin \alpha - \sin^3 \alpha$

2. $\sin 4\alpha = 4 \sin \alpha \cdot \cos \alpha - 8 \sin^3 \alpha \cdot \cos \alpha$

3. $\sin 5\alpha = 5 \sin \alpha - 20 \sin^3 \alpha + 16 \sin^5 \alpha$

4. $\cos 3\alpha = 4 \cos^3 \alpha - 3 \cos \alpha = \cos^3 \alpha - 3 \cos \alpha \cdot \sin^2 \alpha$

5. $\cos 4\alpha = 8 \cos^4 \alpha - 8 \cos^2 \alpha + 1$

6. $\cos 5\alpha = 16 \cos^5 \alpha - 20 \cos^3 \alpha + 5 \cos \alpha$

7. $\tan 3\alpha = \dfrac{3 \tan \alpha - \tan^3 \alpha}{1 - 3 \tan^2 \alpha}$

8. $\tan 4\alpha = \dfrac{4 \tan \alpha - 4 \tan^3 \alpha}{1 - 6 \tan^2 \alpha + \tan^4 \alpha}$

9. $\tan 5\alpha = \dfrac{\tan^5 \alpha - 10 \tan^3 \alpha + 5 \tan \alpha}{1 - 10 \tan^2 \alpha + 5 \tan^4 \alpha}$

10. $\cot 3\alpha = \dfrac{\cot^3 \alpha - 3 \cot \alpha}{3 \cot^2 \alpha - 1}$

11. $\cot 4\alpha = \dfrac{1 - 6 \tan^2 \alpha + \tan^4 \alpha}{4 \tan \alpha - 4 \tan^3 \alpha}$

12. $\cot 5\alpha = \dfrac{1 - 10 \tan^2 \alpha + 5 \tan^4 \alpha}{\tan^5 \alpha - 10 \tan^3 \alpha + 5 \tan \alpha}$

Half Angle Formulas

1. $\sin \dfrac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1 - \cos \alpha}{2}}$

2. $\cos \dfrac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \cos \alpha}{2}}$

3. $\tan \dfrac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1 - \cos \alpha}{1 + \cos \alpha}} = \dfrac{\sin \alpha}{1 + \cos \alpha} = \dfrac{1 - \cos \alpha}{\sin \alpha} = \csc \alpha - \cot \alpha$

4. $\cot \dfrac{\alpha}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \cos \alpha}{1 - \cos \alpha}} = \dfrac{\sin \alpha}{1 - \cos \alpha} = \dfrac{1 + \cos \alpha}{\sin \alpha} = \csc \alpha + \cot \alpha$

Half Angle Tangent Identities

1. $\sin \alpha = \dfrac{2 \tan \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1 + \tan^2 \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

2. $\cos \alpha = \dfrac{1 - \tan^2 \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1 + \tan^2 \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

3. $\tan \alpha = \dfrac{2 \tan \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{1 - \tan^2 \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

4. $\cot \alpha = \dfrac{1 - \tan^2 \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}{2 \tan \dfrac{\alpha}{2}}$

Transforming of Trigonometric Expressions to Product

1. $\sin \alpha + \sin \beta = 2 \sin \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \cos \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$

2. $\sin \alpha - \sin \beta = 2 \cos \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \sin \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$

3. $\cos \alpha + \cos \beta = 2 \cos \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \cos \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$

4. $\cos \alpha - \cos \beta = -2 \sin \dfrac{\alpha + \beta}{2} \sin \dfrac{\alpha - \beta}{2}$

5. $\tan \alpha + \tan \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\alpha+\beta)}{\cos \alpha \cdot \cos \beta}$

6. $\tan \alpha - \tan \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\alpha-\beta)}{\cos \alpha \cdot \cos \beta}$

7. $\cot \alpha + \cot \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\beta+\alpha)}{\sin \alpha \cdot \sin \beta}$

8. $\cot \alpha - \cot \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\beta-\alpha)}{\sin \alpha \cdot \sin \beta}$

9. $\cos \alpha + \sin \alpha = \sqrt{2}\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\alpha\right) = \sqrt{2}\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}+\alpha\right)$

10. $\cos \alpha - \sin \alpha = \sqrt{2}\sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\alpha\right) = \sqrt{2}\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}+\alpha\right)$

11. $\tan \alpha + \cot \beta = \dfrac{\cos(\alpha-\beta)}{\cos \alpha \cdot \sin \beta}$

12. $\tan \alpha - \cot \beta = -\dfrac{\cos(\alpha+\beta)}{\cos \alpha \cdot \sin \beta}$

13. $1 + \cos \alpha = 2 \cos^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}$

14. $1 - \cos \alpha = 2 \sin^{2}\dfrac{\alpha}{2}$

15. $1 + \sin \alpha = 2 \cos^{2}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\dfrac{\alpha}{2}\right)$

16. $1 - \sin \alpha = 2 \sin^{2}\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}-\dfrac{\alpha}{2}\right)$

Transforming of Trigonometric Expressions to Sum

1. $\sin \alpha \cdot \sin \beta = \dfrac{\cos(\alpha-\beta) - \cos(\alpha+\beta)}{2}$

2. $\cos \alpha \cdot \cos \beta = \dfrac{\cos(\alpha-\beta) + \cos(\alpha+\beta)}{2}$

3. $\sin \alpha \cdot \cos \beta = \dfrac{\sin(\alpha-\beta) + \sin(\alpha+\beta)}{2}$

4. $\tan \alpha \cdot \tan \beta = \dfrac{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}{\cot \alpha + \cot \beta}$

5. $\cot \alpha \cdot \cot \beta = \dfrac{\cot \alpha + \cot \beta}{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}$

6. $\tan \alpha \cdot \cot \beta = \dfrac{\tan \alpha + \cot \beta}{\cot \alpha + \tan \beta}$

Powers of Trigonometric Functions

1. $\sin^2\alpha = \dfrac{1-\cos 2\alpha}{2}$

2. $\sin^3\alpha = \dfrac{3 \sin \alpha - \sin 3\alpha}{4}$

3. $\sin^4\alpha = \dfrac{\cos 4\alpha - 4 \cos 2\alpha + 3}{8}$

4. $\sin^5\alpha = \dfrac{10 \sin \alpha - 5 \sin 3\alpha + \sin 5\alpha}{16}$

5. $\sin^6\alpha = \dfrac{10 - 15 \cos 2\alpha + 6 \cos 4\alpha - \cos 6\alpha}{32}$

6. $\cos^2\alpha = \dfrac{1+\cos 2\alpha}{2}$

7. $\cos^3\alpha = \dfrac{3 \cos \alpha + \cos 3\alpha}{4}$

8. $\cos^4\alpha = \dfrac{\cos 4\alpha + 4 \cos 2\alpha + 3}{8}$

9. $\cos^5\alpha = \dfrac{10 \cos \alpha + 5 \sin 3\alpha + \cos 5\alpha}{16}$

10. $\cos^6\alpha = \dfrac{10 + 15 \cos 2\alpha + 6 \cos 4\alpha + \cos 6\alpha}{32}$
